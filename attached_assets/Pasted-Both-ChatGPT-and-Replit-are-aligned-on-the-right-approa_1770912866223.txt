B. Replit's "5-10% Influence" Is Too Conservative

My take: 5-10% is invisible.

Math:

Person A: fame_index = 500,000

Better threshold: 15-25% influence on final score, but cap absolute rank movement (no more than ±3 ranks per 10min cycle).

This creates visible ticks without chaos.
