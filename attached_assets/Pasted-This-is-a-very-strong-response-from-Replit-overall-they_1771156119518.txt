Why Hot Movers was still changing often (and should now stop)

If Hot Movers was flickering every few minutes, it was almost certainly because:

baseline snapshot selection was sliding, OR

thresholds were computed on slightly different populations each request, OR

your “24h change” values were unstable because baseline was unstable

Now that it’s run-based, Hot Movers should only change when a new ingestion run completes (unless your fast-lane injects internal signals into that endpoint, which it shouldn’t).

So your sanity test is:

✅ Refresh 20 times in 10 minutes → Hot Movers should remain identical.
✅ Wait until after next ingestion completes → Hot Movers may change.
