Two important follow-ups I’d ask Replit to sanity-check (worth doing now)
1) Security / privacy on /api/me/predictions

Make sure it is requireAuth and only returns the logged-in user’s bets (no userId param that could be spoofed).
Also ensure it doesn’t leak anything sensitive (emails, internal IDs beyond what you need).

2) Result mapping correctness on cards

Edge cases to confirm:

Void/refund shows “Refunded” and stake returned, not “Lost”.

Pending stays pending until settled (don’t show “Closed” without outcome).

If user placed multiple bets on same market (allowed?), decide: show latest bet, aggregate, or show “Multiple positions”.
