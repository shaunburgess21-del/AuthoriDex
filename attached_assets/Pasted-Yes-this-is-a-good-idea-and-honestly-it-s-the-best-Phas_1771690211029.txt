This is the best part.

fast

Two things to watch:

Versioning: add something like diagnostics.version = 2 or driversModel = "velocity_components_v1" so you know what logic produced it.

Old snapshots: you’ll see (estimate) for about 24 hours until enough new snapshots exist. That’s normal and totally acceptable.

The only “red flag”

Make sure the UI doesn’t flip-flop too much between “estimate” and “exact” per person.

If it feels jumpy, use a rule:

only show “exact” once you have two exact snapshots in the 20–28h window

otherwise keep estimate until stable
