"Audit Everything": Absolutely yes. You don't just want to know who moved the money; you want to know which admin changed Elon Musk's photo to a potato at 2 AM. A blanket rule covers that.

"Wrapper Logic": Also yes. Even though we know the endpoints exist, explicitly telling the AI "Don't invent new logic" prevents it from accidentally writing a second scoring engine that conflicts with the first.
